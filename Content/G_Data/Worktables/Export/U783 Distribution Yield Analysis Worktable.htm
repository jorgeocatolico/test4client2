U783: Distribution Yield Analysis

The standard export worktables are used by the pricing process to store or export all values that may be required for follow-on reporting, analysis or processing purposes. The information stored in export worktables cannot be updated by a user and represents a permanent record of calculated values or values that were used in a process.
