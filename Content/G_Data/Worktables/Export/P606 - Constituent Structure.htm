P606: Constituent Structure

The NAV Oversight process calculates one or more price records. The values calculated along with details of the value types, such as constituent type, are recorded in the P606 worktable.

This worktable is populated when the [EX300] - Export Constituent Structure function is selected in the 'Exports' phase of a pricing process template.
