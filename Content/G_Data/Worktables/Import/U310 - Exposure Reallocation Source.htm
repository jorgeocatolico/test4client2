U310: Exposure Reallocation Source

The U310: Exposure Reallocation Source worktable contains details of the assets, and which type of value is to be reallocated to other assets during a Cash Allocation or Rebalancing process.

Note

For further detail on the reallocation of values within the Portfolio Management product, refer to Reallocation of Value.

 Sample Worktable Data

Processes that use this Worktable

This worktable is used by Portfolio Management. The assets and their values which are to be reallocated to other entities during a Cash Allocation or Rebalancing process are stored here.
