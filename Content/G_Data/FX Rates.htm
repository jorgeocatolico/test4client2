Internal FX Rates

pControl has the capability to receive FX rates from external vendors, an external investment management system or other external sources into the U178: FX Rates import worktable. FX rates are validated through a process called Valuation 1 process. Once validated, the FX rates are stored internally and pControl then generates all cross FX rates.

Various pControl modules utilise the internal FX rates in their calculations (e.g. Orders, Reconciliations, NAV Control).

To view the FX rates select Data > FX Rates.

Note

When the date range to view FX rates is greater than 45 days, pControl will display a warning message and auto adjust the display date range to 45 days calculated from the 'To' date.
