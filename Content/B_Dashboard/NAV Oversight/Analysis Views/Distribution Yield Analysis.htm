Distribution Yield Analysis

A fund's distribution yield is a measure of the income return of a fund. This yield is expressed as a percentage based on the income amount per share divided by the share's net asset value. Fund yields vary with the fund's market value and changes to the annual dividend distribution. The distribution yield is calculated by taking the most recent distribution i.e. current day, divided by the shares of the fund, then divided by the NAV price of the fund, and then multiplying it by the number of days in the year to annualise it. The annualised total is reported as a percentage.

Distribution Yield Analysis (DYAS) provides a day over day or period over period view into the components and impacts of a pool or class' distribution yield in a framework that is similar to NAV Movement Analysis, which then provides a means to compare the performance of funds. The distribution yield analysis is performed on days that the fund or share class distributes; and for income share-classes (i.e. not for accumulation share classes) or at pool level if a fund is not defined as either Income or Accumulative. A change in Distribution Yield can be explained by the change in:

  • Shares Outstanding
  • NAV Price
  • and Net Distributed Income.

The change in Net Distributed Income can be further broken down based on account balances at the fund or share class level and applying a tolerance; a breach of tolerance may assist in the understanding of the change in yield.

The DYAS view displays the distribution yield components based on general ledger accounts i.e. the U104: General Ledger. The view is is accessible following the navigation described in Using the Analysis Views from the Exceptions screen, or via the Fund Overview ellipsis on the Fund Sign Off screen.

DYAS Report/View

Worktables

The DYAS utilises NAV prices as well as values and mappings from the following worktables. Examples of data mappings are provided in Data Mapping for Analysis Views.

U101: External Unit Prices

The U101: External Unit Prices worktable provides the NAV price for the DYAS view.

 

 U104: General Ledger

The U104: General Ledger worktable contains items and values that will be loaded to pControl accounts, for example, from an external general ledger or report. The DYAS report/view consists of categories representing accounts from the U104: General Ledger, in addition to the categories for Yield, Shares Outstanding and Net Distributed Income.

 

 U108: Units on Issue

The DYAS utilises the current and previous day confirmed paid units as part of the calculations. Calculations are described in full in DYAS Specification Tables.

 

 L315: Account Code Mapping

 

 L184: Account Category

The L184: Account Category maps the account group to the DYAS category. Only categories that have "Distribution Yield Attribution" identified in the L184 category column, will be included in the DYAS view. Setting the category field associates the account groups and linked category codes to DYAS.

U350: Category Hierarchy

 

 U783: Distribution Yield Analysis

This export worktable is described under U783: Distribution Yield Analysis.
