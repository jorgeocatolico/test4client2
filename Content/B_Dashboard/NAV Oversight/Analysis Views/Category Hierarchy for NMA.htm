Category Hierarchy for NMA

The Category Hierarchy used by the NMA report, as shown below, is configured in the U350: Category Hierarchy worktable, and is used to order the categories within the report.

The categories under the AST (Investment Assets Category) are used to categorise assets held by an entity in the U100: Investment Holdings worktable. The category assigned to an asset then impacts how that asset is associated to a benchmark for certain validations. This mapping is maintained in the L235: Benchmark Mapping worktable.

Note

For further detail on L235: Benchmark Mappings worktable, refer to the Asset Level Validations User Guide. For further detail on updating categories under 'AST', refer to the NMA User Guide.
