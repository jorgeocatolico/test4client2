Cash Forecasting

The cash forecasting product provides a summarised view of an organisation’s cash forecasts, based on the cash events of underlying order transactions generated by the Order Management process. Blank values indicate the forecast value is zero. Negative cash balances are highlighted in red when selected. These could indicate cash is being carried forward, failed settlements, or a balance difference between the bank account and the pControl value.

Where differences exist between an entity’s bank account balance, (received from an external source) and pControl’s values, a ‘Balance Difference’ transaction can be created, in 's processing module, to reconcile the difference.

 Financial Events excluded from the Cash Forecast

Financial event transactions associated to a ‘settlement instructions’ entity where the ‘Exclude from Cash Forecast’ attribute is selected will not be displayed in the Cash Forecast view.
