Currency

The currency column contains the currency type of the underlying financial event/s. A blue triangle in the top right hand corner of a forecast value indicates the underlying financial events have been executed in multiple currencies. Hovering over these cells will display the latest foreign exchange rates used to convert the settlement value into a different currency.

Note

The FX rate/s displayed in the pop up box are the latest rates available within pControl. These may be different to the rates used on the settlement date.
