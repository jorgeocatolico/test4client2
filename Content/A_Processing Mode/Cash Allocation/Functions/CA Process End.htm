Deal Consolidation

Processing Phase

Process End Validations

Description

Deal consolidation is the process of updating the value of trades into a base fund to match the trade that base fund eventually places into the market. Confirmation of that external trade will result in the constituent trades also being confirmed.

This function enables the deal consolidator entity to amalgamate all trades generated from its originators into a fund and keep track of any adjustments required to the incoming trades as a result of changes made to the external trade. Thus, if the proposed orders for a deal consolidator entity are modified, then the incoming orders for the entity are amended by the adjustment value. This will maintain a zero cash impact from the cash allocation process.

 Export Cash Allocation Orders

Processing Phase

Process End Validations

Description

This function will export the detailed proposed orders created at the end of the cash allocation process into U570: Cash Allocation Orders.

Both internal and external orders will be exported, i.e. orders between pControl funds and also orders to external managers. The external orders together with their settlement instructions may also be present in P510: Message Interface buffer.

Only Registry Orders that are created during a CAR process and the Approved proposed orders that result in actual orders will be exported to the buffer. Rejected proposed orders as well as orders created outside of cash allocation will not be exported.

Note: Changes to orders, including cancelling orders or re running of cash allocation process will not affect the orders that have already been exported to the U570 worktable.

The export process will only take place after proposed orders are approved or rejected and the actual orders have been created. Any mismatch orders created after deal consolidation and deal mismatch processing will also be exported.

Note: The process will wait for the real orders to be created before continuing to run. The export will occur after the process comes out of its waiting state.
